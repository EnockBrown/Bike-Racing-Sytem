Who We Are

One of the most convinient and reliable rental bike service of a campus with a public bike rental service. The system allows flexibility and convenience for students in the campus, who need to spend their leisure time racing with their friends. It also offers services to non-students which include non-teaching staff and also the citicens. The project includes more than 70 bicycles and several rental bicycles point in the University open area. They also have two points of transport card sales and recharge. The campus students can accede to bikes with no interruption from Sunday to Sunday while Citizens and non-teaching staff can accede to bikes with no interupption from Moonday to Friday.
